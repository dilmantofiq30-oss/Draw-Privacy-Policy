For a better experience and to ensure platform safety, the Service Provider may require you to provide certain personally identifiable and sensitive information to prove human identity and verify that the user is over 18 years of age. This includes, but is not limited to:

The Service Provider provides a clear and transparent pathway for users to delete their accounts and all associated historical data.
